# Our Rules

The complete list of Discord, voice chat, shift, and roleplay rules enforced in Valley Correctional Facility. **Read this before joining any community activity.**

:::danger Ignorance is not a defense
Claiming that you did not know a rule is **not** an acceptable defense for breaking it.
:::

## General Community Conduct

### Discrimination & Hate Speech
Zero tolerance. Discrimination based on protected characteristics is prohibited, including slurs, derogatory jokes, and refusing to interact with members on that basis.

### Terms of Service Violations
Platform ToS compliance is mandatory across Discord and Roblox. Serious violations — involving minors or real-world violence threats — result in an **immediate permanent ban** regardless of history.

### Respect & Common Courtesy
Treat others respectfully. Harassment, bullying, and public mockery are prohibited.

### Profanity & Derogatory Language
General profanity is acceptable in moderation, but using profanity or slurs **as insults** is forbidden.

### NSFW & Graphic Content
Sexual content, gore imagery, and insensitive tragedy jokes are prohibited.

### Real-World Tragedies & Politics
Respectful discussion of events is allowed; **partisan politics and political figures are not**.

### Disclosure of Classified Information
Sharing or claiming access to confidential VCF information is prohibited. Failing to report a known breach is also a violation.

## Discord-Specific Rules

- **Language** — English-only, so moderation stays effective.
- **Malicious Links** — suspicious URLs and shorteners are prohibited.
- **Self-Promotion** — no advertising without staff permission.
- **Channel Misuse** — use channels appropriately; tickets are for serious inquiries only.
- **Spamming** — no flood messaging or mass mentions.
- **Staff DMs** — conduct rules apply in DMs; harassment of staff is reportable.
- **Voice Conduct** — all text rules apply; Activities require moderator supervision.

## In-Game Roleplay Rules

> For the full guidance, see the [Roleplay Information](/shifts/roleplay-information) page.

- **Fail Roleplay** — unrealistic actions that break immersion are forbidden.
- **Forbidden Themes** — sexual RP, graphic torture, and large-scale attack scenarios are prohibited.
- **Powergaming & Metagaming** — forcing outcomes and using OOC knowledge for IC advantage are forbidden.
- **Intrusion & Trolling** — disrupting scenes and provoking staff is prohibited.
- **Voice Chat** — in-character communication only.
- **Unauthorized Team Access** — joining whitelisted teams (CO, Medical, Sheriff) without approval is a serious offense.
- **Staff Compliance** — direct moderator orders must be obeyed; non-compliance is reportable.

# Roleplay Information

Guidelines for roleplay during VCF shifts — covering hostage situations, baiting, powergaming, metagaming, and the standards expected of every participant.

## The Core Principle

> Roleplay is a two-way street. Your actions should create engaging stories **with** other players, not **against** them.

Prioritise the health of the scene over personal victory.

## Key Distinctions

**In-Character (IC) vs. Out-of-Character (OOC).** Keep separate what your character knows (IC) from player knowledge such as Discord discussions or usernames (OOC).

**Character boundaries.** Your role is distinct from you as a player. Criticism of your character's actions is not personal criticism of you.

## Recommended Scenarios

### Medical Scenes
Minor ailments, injuries from falls, and routine infirmary visits work well. **Staff approval is required** for graphic content or disease outbreaks.

### Hostage Situations
A **single hostage** with realistic, negotiable demands (vehicle access, yard passage) creates engaging tension. Multiple hostages or absurd demands undermine the scenario.

### Conflict & Disobedience
Characters may plausibly refuse orders in line with their personality — realism, not blanket defiance.

## Forbidden Mechanics

:::danger Do not do these
- **Fear Roleplay** — realistically acknowledge threats. An unarmed inmate dismissing three armed officers violates this.
- **Powergaming** — roleplay attempts, not guaranteed outcomes. Use "*attempts to tackle*" rather than "*tackles and succeeds*".
- **Metagaming** — never use OOC knowledge (a riot planned in Discord, a player's username) for IC advantage.
- **Baiting & Trolling** — no contraband flashing, noise spam, or disrupting scenes.
- **Scene Intrusion** — do not force yourself into private roleplay moments.
:::

### Do / Don't at a glance

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Roleplay an *attempt* and let the dice fall | Declare an outcome your target can't counter |
| React believably to danger | Ignore weapons pointed at you |
| Keep Discord chatter out of the scene | Act on OOC information in-character |
| Ask before joining an ongoing scene | Barge into others' private roleplay |

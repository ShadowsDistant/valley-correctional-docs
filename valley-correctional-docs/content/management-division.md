# Management Division

> The Management Division guides, supports, and directs every operational division so that each functions effectively and cohesively.

## Core Principles

### Leadership & Accountability
Managers are held to the highest standard of conduct. Their role is to mentor, support, and guide their teams so that community goals are consistently met.

### Strategic Oversight
The Management Division provides direction and clears obstacles for staff. Managers are experts in their division's operations and continuously develop strategies for improvement.

### Inter-Divisional Collaboration
No division operates in isolation. Managers work closely with one another to ensure smooth communication, resolve friction, and deliver a seamless experience for the community.

## Areas of Oversight

| Division | Responsibility |
| --- | --- |
| **Moderation Division** | Rule enforcement, moderator recruitment & training |
| **Specialized Investigations Division** | Investigative standards and confidentiality protocols |
| **Prison Administration Division** | Department of Corrections operations and shift management |
| **Development Division** | Development lifecycle and technical project delivery |

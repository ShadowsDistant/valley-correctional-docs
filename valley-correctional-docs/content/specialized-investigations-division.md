# Specialized Investigations Division

The Specialized Investigations Division (SID) maintains staff integrity by investigating misconduct and ensuring personnel adhere to established standards.

## Our Mission

Investigators operate with **impartiality, thoroughness, and discretion**, holding all personnel to a strict code of conduct.

## Jurisdiction

SID has jurisdiction over six primary violation categories:

1. **Command Abuse** — misusing administrative tools beyond their intended purpose.
2. **Biased Moderation** — allowing personal bias to influence administrative decisions.
3. **Community Standards Violations** — staff breaching established community rules.
4. **Confidential Information Disclosure** — unauthorized sharing of classified information (**zero tolerance**).
5. **Failure to Moderate** — ignoring rule violations or applying inconsistent punishments.
6. **Mismanagement** — supervisory failures affecting operations or team morale.

## Reporting Process

Staff misconduct can be reported **confidentially** through a dedicated Discord ticket channel.

:::warning Whistleblower protection
Reporter identity is **never** shared with the subject of an investigation. Whistleblower protections are guaranteed, and retaliation is classified as a serious offense.
:::

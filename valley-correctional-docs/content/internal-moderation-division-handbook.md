## Core Responsibilities

Consistent failure to perform these duties constitutes a **Failure to Moderate** and may be investigated by SID.

:::details Uphold Rules & Policies
Proactively enforce all community rules, Valley Prison rules, Roblox Community Standards, and Discord's ToS.
:::

:::details In-Game & Discord Moderation
Actively moderate official shifts and the community Discord server.
:::

:::details Ticket Management
Respond to and resolve user support tickets. **SLA:** initial response within **48 hours**, full resolution within **7 days** where possible.
:::

:::details Appeals Processing (Senior Moderator+)
Handle ban appeals impartially, reviewing all evidence before deciding.
:::

:::details Community Engagement
Engage professionally and helpfully with community members.
:::

:::details Following Directives
Execute all lawful directives from supervisory staff in a timely manner.
:::

## Promotion & Demotion Policy

:::details Promotion Criteria
All promotions require Internal Operations Manager approval.

| From | To | Requirements |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Junior Moderator | Moderator | 1 month active tenure; satisfactory Melonly record; no active infractions; demonstrated professionalism |
| Moderator | Senior Moderator | 3 months as Moderator; consistent enforcement quality; leadership conduct; no active infractions |

> **Note:** promotions are not automatic — tenure alone does not guarantee advancement.
:::

:::details Demotion Criteria
- **Performance Demotion:** failing activity quotas or consistently poor enforcement.
- **Conduct Demotion:** a serious violation not warranting termination.
- **Process:** Internal Operations Manager approval required, logged in Melonly. 7 calendar days to appeal.
:::

## Probationary Period Policy

:::details Probationary Moderator Status
All newly promoted Moderators begin a **14-day Probationary Period**.

**During probation:**
- Full moderation commands available.
- Major decisions (bans, permanent mutes) must be reviewed by a Senior Moderator or Manager.
- Any command abuse or serious conduct violation results in immediate removal.

**After probation:** the Internal Operations Manager confirms or reverses the promotion.
:::

## `;give` Command Authorization

:::details Authorized Use of the ;give Command
The `;give` command may only be used for documented roleplay purposes with prior written Manager approval.

1. **Request** — Submit a ticket in the designated staff channel: player receiving the item, item name, roleplay justification, approving Manager's name.
2. **Manager Approval** — A Manager posts approval in the ticket thread. Unapproved requests must not be actioned.
3. **Execution & Logging** — After execution, log the command in the staff command log, including the approving Manager's name and reason.

> **⚠️ Warning:** unauthorized use of `;give` constitutes **Command Abuse** and is referred to SID.
:::

## Verbal Warning Logging

:::details Logging Verbal Warnings
All verbal warnings — including in-shift warnings for FRP and powergaming — must be logged.

- Create a **Note** on the member's Melonly profile with date, violation, and brief description.
- Or log to the staff log channel as: `[DATE] — Verbal Warning — [USER] — [VIOLATION] — [DESCRIPTION]`

> **⚠️ Warning:** unlogged verbal warnings cannot be used to escalate punishments and expire after **30 days**.
:::

## Conflict of Interest Escalation

:::details COI Escalation Path
When a moderator has a personal relationship with a subject of enforcement:

1. **Peer Moderator** — Hand off to the next available moderator on shift.
2. **Senior Moderator** — If the peer is also compromised, escalate to a Senior Moderator.
3. **SID Referral** — If Senior Moderators are compromised, escalate directly to SID.
:::

## Code of Conduct

### Professionalism & Respect
- Respectful, calm tone in all interactions.
- No derogatory, demeaning, or inflammatory language.
- No public discussion of controversial or NSFW topics.
- Conduct rules apply to DMs with community members.

### Impartiality & Fairness
- Biased Moderation is strictly prohibited.
- Decisions are based on evidence and rules, not friendships or feelings.

### Requesting an LOA

:::details LOA Rules & Guidelines
- **Duration:** minimum 3 days, maximum 14 days (up to 31 days with prior manager approval).
- **Cooldown:** denied requests have a 3-day cooldown before resubmission.
- **Valid Reasons:** family, exams, medical leave, mental health.
- **Invalid Reasons:** boredom, grounded, temporary tech issues.

## Punishment Rubrics

### General & Discord Conduct

:::warning ToS violations require SID escalation
All confirmed Terms of Service violations on Discord or Roblox must be logged and escalated to SID, even when the moderation action (ban, mute) is already applied. SID maintains the cross-platform reporting record for escalations to Discord Trust & Safety, Roblox moderation, or Valley Prison staff.
:::

| Violation | First Offense | Second Offense | Third Offense | Severity Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **ToS Violations (Discord/Roblox)** | Permanent Ban | — | — | High to Zero-Tolerance. Severe violations (CSAM, real-world threats, ban evasion, malware) are immediate permanent bans on first offense. |
| **Harassment & Bullying** | Formal Warning & Mute (1–24h) | Temporary Ban (3–7 days) | Permanent Ban | High — severe first offenses may warrant immediate ban |
| **Disclosure of Classified Info** | Temporary Ban (7–14 days) | Permanent Ban | — | High — malicious leaks = immediate permanent ban |
| **Sharing PII** | Kick & Formal Warning | Permanent Ban | — | High — escalates with malicious intent |
| **Profanity as Insult** | Formal Warning & Mute (12h) | Mute (24h) or Temp Ban (3d) | Temp Ban (7+ days) | Medium — context matters |
| **Spamming / Mass Mentioning** | Formal Warning | Mute (1–12h) | Mute (24h) or Kick | Low |
| **Channel Misuse** | Verbal Reminder or Warning | Mute (1h) | Mute (12h) | Low |
| **Self-Promotion / Advertising** | Formal Warning & Message Delete | Kick | Temp Ban (3 days) | Medium — DM poaching may warrant first-offense temp ban |
| **VC Misuse** | Mute (1h) or Kick from VC | Temp Mute (24h) | Temp Ban (1 day) | Low to Medium |

# ToS Enforcement Changes

We recently deployed **TASE**, a Discord moderation bot that helps identify and remove users involved with Roblox "condos." These activities violate Roblox's Terms of Service and frequently involve underage users. To protect our community, TASE is now active and will be used to enforce bans according to the criteria below.

---

## What TASE Does

- **Detects accounts linked to condo communities.**
- **Issues bans** when users meet our enforcement thresholds.
- **Sends an explanatory DM** with appeal instructions when a ban is applied.

---

## Enforcement Criteria

| Criterion | What it means |
| --- | --- |
| **Present in more than one condo Discord server** | User appears in multiple servers known for condo activity |
| **Staff member or server booster in a condo server** | User holds a position of influence or support in a condo server |
| **Threat level Critical, High, or Medium** | TASE risk assessment flags the account at these levels |
| **Score over 200** | User score exceeds 200 (this is 50 points below the permanent, unappealable ban threshold) |

---

## What to Expect

- We have already banned **over 120 users** and will continue enforcement.
- **At the end of the month** we will remove the staged criteria and ban **anyone involved with condos**, regardless of degree of involvement.
- If you are banned from **Valley Correctional Facility**, you will receive a DM from **TASE** explaining the reason and how to appeal.
- **Appeals must be submitted through TASE.** We will not accept appeals submitted directly to server staff. You can appeal via the TASE instructions or through this invite: [discord.gg/VH4e8Wxfmd](https://discord.gg/VH4e8Wxfmd).

---

## Next Steps and Recommendations

- **If you are in any condo server, leave now.** We will not tolerate behavior that harms children or violates platform rules.
- If you believe a ban was applied in error, follow the appeal instructions sent by TASE.
- For questions about enforcement or community safety, contact server moderators through the usual channels.

**Thank you** for helping keep our community safe and compliant with Roblox ToS.

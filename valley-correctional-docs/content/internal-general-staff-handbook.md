# General Staff Handbook

> Baseline conduct standards applicable to all VCF staff members across every division.

:::warning Internal — staff only
This handbook is restricted to logged-in VCF staff. It operates **alongside** division-specific handbooks, which expand rather than override these rules.
:::

## Core Expectations

- **Professional conduct** across all channels at all times.
- **Meet activity quotas** set by your division.
- **Enforce rules impartially**, without favoritism.
- **Maintain strict confidentiality** regarding internal matters — an obligation that **persists after departure**.

## Critical Termination Triggers

:::danger Immediate removal — no appeal
Several violations warrant immediate removal without appeal:

- **At-will removal** by the Lead Overseer or Community Manager for any reason.
- Association with banned individuals or documented drama-starters (requires disclosure of pre-existing relationships).
- Organizing strikes, walkouts, or coordinated departure threats.
- Behavior suggestive of leaking internal information.
- Discussing internal issues with non-staff members.
- Weaponizing faction reports to pressure leadership.
- Disclosing confidential staff information.
- Engaging in direct threats to VCF operations.
:::

## Standard Violations

Other misconduct falls under the **SID point system**, where violations accumulate over a rolling six-month window toward disciplinary thresholds. See the [SID Handbook](/internal-documents/specialized-investigations-division-handbook) for the full point matrix and thresholds.

## Whistleblower Protections

Reports can be filed through:

- **SID tickets** (confidential),
- **Direct message to leadership**, or
- **Anonymously**.

:::warning Retaliation is a Tier 3 offense
Retaliation against a reporter constitutes a **Tier 3 offense** and triggers **immediate termination**. Your identity as a reporter is never shared with the subject of an investigation.
:::

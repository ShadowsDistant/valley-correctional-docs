# Our Rules

These are the complete and binding rules for Valley Correctional Facility, enforced across our Discord server, voice channels, and in-game shifts. **Read them in full before taking part in any community activity.** By participating in VCF, you agree to follow every rule below.

:::danger Two things that apply to every rule
**Ignorance is not a defense.** Not knowing a rule does not excuse breaking it.

**The spirit of the rules governs.** Attempting to exploit a technicality or a loophole to do something a rule is clearly meant to prevent is itself a violation. Staff may act on conduct that plainly harms the community even where it is not spelled out word-for-word below.
:::

## How enforcement works

Violations are grouped into three tiers. Moderators apply punishments proportionately, taking history and intent into account. Severe cases may skip tiers.

| Tier | Nature | Typical outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier 1** | Minor or first-time lapses | Warning, short mute |
| **Tier 2** | Repeated or moderate violations | Extended mute, kick, temporary ban |
| **Tier 3** | Severe violations | Permanent ban, escalation to platform moderation |

You must be old enough to use Discord and Roblox (**13+**) to participate in VCF.

## General Community Conduct

### Discrimination & hate speech
Zero tolerance. Discrimination, slurs, or derogatory content targeting any protected characteristic — including race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, sex, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or age — is a **Tier 3** offense. This includes "jokes," coded language, and refusing to interact with members on a discriminatory basis.

### Terms of Service violations
You must follow the Terms of Service of every platform we use (Discord, Roblox, and Valley Prison). Content involving **minors in a sexual context, credible real-world threats of violence, or similar severe violations results in an immediate, permanent, unappealable ban** regardless of history, and is reported to the relevant platform.

### Respect & harassment
Treat every member with basic courtesy. Harassment, bullying, targeted mockery, brigading, or following a member across channels to provoke them is prohibited. Severity determines the tier.

### Threats, blackmail & doxxing
Threats of any kind, blackmail, extortion, and sharing or threatening to share another person's private information (real name, address, workplace, other accounts, etc.) are **Tier 3** offenses.

### Impersonation
Do not impersonate VCF staff, other members, or any real person or organisation, whether by name, avatar, or claimed authority.

### NSFW & graphic content
Sexual content, gore, shock media, and insensitive "jokes" about real tragedies are prohibited in all channels and in voice.

### Real-world tragedies & politics
Respectful discussion of real-world events is permitted. Partisan politics, campaigning, and debate about political figures or divisive social issues are not.

### Confidentiality
Sharing, leaking, paraphrasing, or claiming access to confidential VCF information is a **Tier 3** offense. If you become aware of a leak or breach, you are required to report it; failing to do so is also a violation.

## Discord Rules

- **English only** — so moderation can be applied consistently.
- **No malicious or deceptive links** — including shorteners, unsolicited invites, IP grabbers, and malware. Posting them is a Tier 3 offense.
- **No advertising or poaching** — do not advertise servers, services, or content, or recruit our members to other communities, in channels or via DMs, without staff permission.
- **Use channels as intended** — keep discussion on-topic; reserve tickets for genuine issues.
- **No spam** — flooding, wall-posting, mass mentions, and disruptive formatting are prohibited.
- **No filter evasion** — bypassing the word filter or automod (spacing, symbols, misspellings, etc.) is treated as if you had posted the underlying content.
- **Staff DMs** — the conduct rules apply in direct messages. Harassing or abusing staff over DM is reportable and actioned.
- **Voice conduct** — all text rules apply in voice. Soundboards, music, and Activities require moderator supervision.

## Alternate Accounts & Ban Evasion

- Attempting to **evade a mute, ban, or blacklist** using an alternate account, a new account, or another platform is a **Tier 3** offense and results in a permanent ban of all associated accounts.
- Do not use alternate accounts to gain an unfair advantage — for example, to influence applications, votes, giveaways, or to bypass any restriction.

## In-Game Roleplay Rules

The following summarises in-game conduct. See the full **[Roleplay Information](/shifts/roleplay-information)** page for detailed guidance and examples.

- **Fail Roleplay (FRP)** — unrealistic actions that break immersion are prohibited.
- **Forbidden themes** — sexual roleplay, graphic torture, and large-scale terrorism/attack scenarios are prohibited.
- **Powergaming** — forcing outcomes on others instead of roleplaying an attempt is prohibited.
- **Metagaming** — using out-of-character knowledge (Discord chatter, usernames, streams) for in-character advantage is prohibited.
- **Baiting, trolling & scene intrusion** — deliberately disrupting scenes or provoking staff is prohibited.
- **Exploiting & cheating** — using exploits, glitches, or third-party tools, or abusing bugs to gain access or advantage, is a Tier 3 offense.
- **Unauthorized team access** — joining a whitelisted team (Corrections, Medical, Sheriff's Department, CERT, etc.) without approval is a serious offense.
- **Staff compliance** — lawful instructions from a moderator on shift must be followed. Non-compliance is reportable; you may raise concerns afterward through a ticket.

## Reporting, Appeals & Integrity

- **Report through the proper channels** — open a ticket or use the report tools. Do not take enforcement into your own hands.
- **False reports & evidence tampering** — knowingly filing a false report, or destroying, altering, or fabricating evidence, is a Tier 2–3 offense.
- **Retaliation** — retaliating against anyone for reporting a rule violation, in good faith, is a **Tier 3** offense.
- **Appeals** — bans and infractions may be appealed through the official process referenced in the moderation message (see the [Community Information](/information) page). Being abusive during an appeal, or spamming appeals, forfeits it.

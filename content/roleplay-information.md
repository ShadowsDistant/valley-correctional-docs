# Roleplay Information

These guidelines set the standard for roleplay during VCF shifts. They exist to keep scenes fair, immersive, and enjoyable for everyone involved.

## The Core Principle

> Roleplay is a two-way street. Your actions should create engaging stories **with** other players, not **against** them.

Always prioritise the health of the scene over a personal "win."

## In-Character vs. Out-of-Character

- **In-character (IC)** is what your character knows and does within the roleplay.
- **Out-of-character (OOC)** is real-world knowledge — Discord discussions, usernames, streams, and anything said outside the scene.

Keep the two separate. Criticism of your character's actions is not a personal attack on you.

## Recommended Scenarios

### Medical scenes
Minor ailments, injuries from falls, and routine infirmary visits work well. **Staff approval is required** for graphic content or disease outbreaks.

### Hostage situations
A **single hostage** with realistic, negotiable demands (vehicle access, yard passage) creates genuine tension. Multiple hostages or absurd demands undermine the scenario and are not permitted without staff approval.

### Conflict & disobedience
Characters may plausibly refuse orders in line with their personality. Aim for believable friction, not blanket defiance.

## Forbidden Mechanics

:::danger Do not do any of the following
- **Fear Roleplay (FRP)** — you must realistically value your character's life and acknowledge threats. An unarmed inmate ignoring three armed officers is FRP.
- **Powergaming** — roleplay *attempts*, not guaranteed outcomes. Use "*attempts to tackle*", not "*tackles and pins them down*".
- **Metagaming** — never use OOC knowledge (a riot planned in Discord, a player's username) for IC advantage.
- **Baiting & trolling** — no flashing contraband to bait staff, noise spam, or deliberately derailing scenes.
- **Scene intrusion** — do not force your way into a private roleplay moment.
- **Exploiting** — using glitches, exploits, or third-party tools is prohibited and reported to SID.
:::

### Do / Don't at a glance

| Do | Don't |
| :--- | :--- |
| Roleplay an *attempt* and let it play out | Declare an outcome your target can't counter |
| React believably to danger | Ignore weapons pointed at you |
| Keep Discord chatter out of the scene | Act on OOC information in-character |
| Ask before joining an ongoing scene | Barge into others' private roleplay |

Moderator instructions on shift are final. If you disagree, comply first and raise it afterward through a ticket.

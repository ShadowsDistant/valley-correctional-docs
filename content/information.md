# Community Information

Valley Correctional Facility (VCF) is the largest prison-roleplay community for Valley Prison, dedicated to a safe, fun, and engaging experience for every member. This page answers the questions we get asked most.

:::info Where to get help
The **Management Division** oversees VCF, but day-to-day issues should be raised through **support tickets** rather than by direct-messaging a manager. **Community Support** is handled by the Moderation Division — open a ticket or use the Q&A channels for assistance.
:::

## Shifts & Participation

### Where do I find shift links?
Shift links are posted in the **#shifts** channel on our Discord.

### How long does a shift last?
Most shifts run for approximately **1 hour 30 minutes**.

### What timezone do shifts run in?
Shifts primarily operate in the **EST** timezone.

## Careers & Applications

### How do I become a Corrections Officer (CO)?
CO positions require an **application**, which is posted in **#announcements** when recruitment opens.

### How do I join CERT / RIOT?
You must be an **active CO** and pass an **internal tryout**.

### How do I become a Moderator?
Moderator roles require an **application** plus a **voice interview**.

### What is Fremont State Police (FSP)?
FSP is an **independent partner faction** that VCF does not manage. You can join FSP during shifts if you meet their requirements.

## Access & Technical

### I'm on console — how do I join?
Console players need to be **Roblox friends with the server owner**. Access is best-effort due to platform constraints, so we can't always guarantee a slot.

## Factions, Bans & Appeals

### What happened to the Valley County Sheriff's Office (VCSO)?
The VCSO faction was discontinued. VCSO-branded teams now operate under the **Sheriff's Department**.

### How do I appeal a ban?
- **Main game bans** are appealed through the main game's official server.
- **VCF community bans** use the appeal link found in the **Dyno** bot's ban message.
- **Employment blacklists** are reserved for severe cases; appeals route exclusively to the **Community Manager**.

# Community Information

Valley Correctional Facility (VCF) is the largest prison-roleplay community for Valley Prison, dedicated to a safe, fair, and engaging experience for every member. This page answers the questions we are asked most often.

:::info Where to get help
The **Management Division** oversees VCF, but day-to-day issues should be raised through **support tickets** rather than by direct-messaging leadership. Community support is handled by the **Moderation Division** — open a ticket or use the Q&A channels for assistance.
:::

## Shifts & Participation

### Where do I find shift links?
Shift links are posted in the **#shifts** channel on our Discord when a session goes live. **Server Boosters** receive the link 10–15 minutes early.

### How long is a shift?
Most shifts run for approximately **1 hour 30 minutes**.

### What timezone do shifts run in?
Shifts primarily operate in the **Eastern (EST)** timezone. Specific dates and times are announced in **#events**.

## Careers & Applications

### How do I become a Corrections Officer (CO)?
Corrections Officer positions require an **application**, which is announced in **#announcements** whenever recruitment opens.

### How do I join CERT or RIOT?
You must be an **active Corrections Officer** and pass an **internal tryout**.

### How do I become a Moderator?
Moderator recruitment requires an **application** followed by a **voice interview**. See the [Moderation Division](/our-divisions/moderation-division) page for details and the current application status.

### What is Fremont State Police (FSP)?
FSP is an **independent partner faction** — including its SWAT sub-division — that VCF does not manage. You may join FSP during shifts if you meet their own requirements.

## Access & Technical

### I'm on console — how do I join?
Console players must be **Roblox friends with the server owner**. Access is provided on a best-effort basis and cannot be guaranteed due to platform limitations.

## Factions, Bans & Appeals

### What happened to the Valley County Sheriff's Office (VCSO)?
The standalone VCSO faction was discontinued. VCSO-branded teams now operate under the **Sheriff's Department**, which is run by the Moderation Division.

### How do I appeal a ban?
- **Main-game bans** are appealed through the main game's own official server.
- **VCF community bans** are appealed using the link included in the moderation bot's ban message (**Dyno**, or **TASE** for ToS-enforcement bans — see [ToS Enforcement Changes](/public-documents/tos-enforcement-changes)).
- **Employment blacklists** are reserved for severe cases; appeals route exclusively to the **Community Manager**.

Appeals must be made in good faith. Being abusive during an appeal, or repeatedly re-submitting, forfeits it.

# General Staff Handbook

> The baseline conduct standards that apply to **every** Valley Correctional Facility staff member, regardless of division.

:::warning Internal — staff only
This handbook is restricted to logged-in VCF staff. It operates **alongside** your division-specific handbook, which expands on — but never overrides — these rules.
:::

## Scope

Every staff member is bound by this handbook, their division handbook, the [Chain of Command](/internal-documents/chain-of-command), and the public [community rules](/community-rules/our-rules). Where two documents appear to conflict, the stricter standard applies, and the matter should be raised with your Division Manager or the Oversight Committee.

VCF operates across four surfaces: the **Discord** server, live **in-game shifts**, the **Melonly** management platform, and **SID / Oversight** review.

## Core Expectations

- **Professionalism** — conduct yourself professionally on every surface, at all times, including in DMs and while off-duty where your conduct reflects on VCF.
- **Impartiality** — enforce and follow the rules without favoritism. Personal relationships never change an outcome.
- **Activity** — meet the activity quota set by your division and request leave through the proper channel before extended absences.
- **Confidentiality** — protect all internal information. This duty **persists after you leave the team**.

## Confidentiality

Internal documents, investigations, staff discussions, and any information marked or understood to be classified are **strictly confidential**. You may not share, leak, screenshot, copy, paraphrase, or discuss them with anyone outside the authorized staff team.

:::danger Leaking is a terminable offense
Leaking or facilitating access to any internal or classified document — during or after your time on staff — is a **Tier 3 offense** resulting in **immediate termination and a permanent employment blacklist**, with a **permanent community ban** possible on a case-by-case basis, and may be escalated to platform moderation. Every staff member accepts this in the Staff Policy Agreement at first sign-in.
:::

## Integrity Rules (loopholes closed)

- **Chain of command** — route concerns and requests through your immediate chain of command. Bypassing it without valid cause may itself be disciplined. Exceptions: reporting to the Oversight Committee or SID.
- **Conflict of interest** — if you have a personal relationship with the subject of an enforcement action, application, or investigation, you must **recuse yourself** and hand off. Acting anyway is misconduct.
- **Alternate accounts & evasion** — using alternate accounts to circumvent any restriction, influence decisions, or hide conduct is prohibited.
- **No weaponizing process** — filing knowingly false reports, weaponizing faction/appeal systems to pressure leadership, or tampering with evidence is misconduct.
- **Spirit over letter** — attempting to exploit a technicality to defeat the clear intent of a policy is itself a violation.

## Discipline

Misconduct is handled by the [Specialized Investigations Division](/internal-documents/specialized-investigations-division-handbook) under the disciplinary **point system** (a rolling six-month window; see the SID Handbook for thresholds). Certain offenses warrant **immediate termination without appeal**, including:

- At-will removal by the Lead Overseer or Community Manager.
- Leaking or disclosing confidential information (termination + permanent employment blacklist; permanent community ban possible case-by-case).
- Retaliation against a reporter (a Tier 3 offense).
- Organizing strikes, walkouts, or coordinated departure threats.
- Association with banned individuals or documented drama-instigators without disclosure.
- Direct threats to VCF operations.

## Whistleblower Protections

Reports may be filed through an **SID ticket**, a **direct message to leadership**, or **anonymously**.

:::warning Retaliation is a Tier 3 offense
A reporter's identity is never shared with the subject of an investigation. Retaliation triggers **immediate termination**.
:::

## Leaving the Team

When you leave or are removed, your access is revoked within 24 hours and SID may audit your final 30 days of activity. Your confidentiality obligations remain in force indefinitely.

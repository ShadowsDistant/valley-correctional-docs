# Specialized Investigations Division

The Specialized Investigations Division (SID) maintains staff integrity by investigating misconduct impartially and ensuring that every member of the team is held to the standards they are trusted to uphold.

## Our Mission

Investigators operate with **impartiality, thoroughness, and discretion**. Cases are decided on evidence, documented carefully, and handled confidentially from intake to conclusion.

## Jurisdiction

SID has jurisdiction over the following categories of staff misconduct:

1. **Command Abuse** — misusing administrative commands or permissions beyond their intended purpose.
2. **Biased Moderation** — allowing personal bias, friendships, or feelings to influence decisions.
3. **Community Standards Violations** — staff breaking the same rules they enforce.
4. **Confidential Information Disclosure** — unauthorized sharing of internal information (**zero tolerance**).
5. **Failure to Moderate** — ignoring rule violations or applying inconsistent enforcement.
6. **Mismanagement** — supervisory failures that harm operations, morale, or community integrity.

## Reporting Staff Misconduct

Any member — staff or community — may report staff misconduct **confidentially** through a dedicated SID ticket channel.

:::warning Whistleblower protection
Reporter identity is **never** shared with the subject of an investigation. Whistleblower protections are guaranteed, and retaliation against a good-faith reporter is a **Tier 3 offense**.
:::

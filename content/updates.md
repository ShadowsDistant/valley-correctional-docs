# Updates

Changelog of updates to the Valley Correctional Facility documentation site, including new pages, content revisions, and feature additions by version.

## Version 2.0.0 — Documentation overhaul & new platform
`Major Update` · `V2` · Published 7/9/2026

**Content**
- Rewrote **every public and internal document** for a clearer, more professional tone.
- Standardised policy across the site: a single **Tier 1–3 offense framework** on the [rules](/community-rules/our-rules) page that aligns with the staff disciplinary process.
- **Closed rule loopholes** — added explicit rules for alternate accounts and ban evasion, filter/automod evasion, impersonation, exploiting and cheating, false reports and evidence tampering, retaliation, and a "spirit of the rules" clause.

**Platform**
- New official **VCF seal** as the site logo and favicon.
- Redesigned **staff login** and added a self-service **Account** page so staff can change their own password.
- Added a required **staff policy agreement** shown at first sign-in.
- Custom iconography across the site and quieter document watermarking.

## Version 1.3.3 — New rules, clearer FAQs, streamlined roleplay guidance
`Minor Update` · `V1` · Published 4/23/2026 @ 12:00 PM EST

**Additions**
- Added a **Terms of Service Violations** rule to the [rules](/community-rules/our-rules) page covering platform-level violations and the zero-tolerance categories that result in an immediate permanent ban.
- Added a **Staff Direct Messages (DMs)** rule — harassment or abuse of staff over DM is now explicitly a community-rules violation.
- Added a link from the in-game rules section to the full [Roleplay Information](/shifts/roleplay-information) page.

**Changes**
- Rewrote the [rules](/community-rules/our-rules) page for faster reading.
- Clarified that **Fremont State Police (FSP)** is an external partner faction across the rules and [community information](/information) pages.
- Refreshed the [community information](/information) FAQ — shorter, easier to skim.
- Updated the employment blacklist FAQ to reflect that appeals route exclusively to the Community Manager.
- Condensed the [roleplay information](/shifts/roleplay-information) page with clearer "Do / Don't" examples.

**Removals**
- Standalone "Personal Information (PII)" and "Threats, Blackmail & Doxxing" sections — the same conduct is still prohibited under General Community Conduct and Terms of Service.

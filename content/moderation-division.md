# Moderation Division

Overview of the Moderation Division at VCF, including its role in community enforcement, how to report a moderator, and how to apply.

## About Our Moderation Division

### Our Mission
The Moderation Division safeguards the community by upholding our standards and keeping the environment safe, engaging, and welcoming for everyone.

### Rule Enforcement & Conduct
Moderators enforce all rules impartially and professionally. They are held to a strict code of conduct and serve as role models for the rest of the community.

## Ranks

### Junior Moderator
Entry-level rank. Newly promoted moderators begin a **14-day probationary period**, during which major actions (bans, permanent mutes) require Senior Moderator or Manager sign-off.

### Moderator
Confirmed moderator with full access to standard moderation tools across Discord and in-game shifts.

### Senior Moderator
Experienced moderator who handles ban appeals, mentors junior staff, and leads in-shift moderation teams.

## Accountability & Reporting

### Report a Moderator
To report staff misconduct, open a **confidential ticket** with the Specialized Investigations Division (SID).

:::info Whistleblower protection
Your identity as a reporter is **never** shared with the subject of the investigation. Retaliation against whistleblowers is a **Tier 3 offense**.
:::

**File a report:** [Open an SID ticket](https://discord.com/channels/1345804368263385170/1345816347657113702)

# Moderation Division

The Moderation Division safeguards Valley Correctional Facility by upholding our standards and keeping the community safe, engaging, and welcoming for everyone.

## Our Mission

Moderators enforce every rule impartially and professionally. They are held to a strict code of conduct and are expected to serve as role models for the rest of the community. Enforcement is consistent, evidence-based, and never influenced by personal relationships.

## Ranks

### Junior Moderator
Entry-level rank. Newly promoted moderators serve a **14-day probationary period**, during which major actions — bans and permanent mutes — require sign-off from a Senior Moderator or Manager.

### Moderator
A confirmed moderator with full access to standard moderation tools across Discord and in-game shifts.

### Senior Moderator
An experienced moderator who handles ban appeals, mentors junior staff, and leads in-shift moderation teams.

## Accountability & Reporting

Moderators are held to the same rules they enforce. To report a moderator, open a **confidential ticket** with the Specialized Investigations Division (SID).

:::info Whistleblower protection
Your identity as a reporter is **never** shared with the subject of an investigation. Retaliation against anyone who reports staff misconduct in good faith is a **Tier 3 offense**.
:::

**File a report:** [Open an SID ticket](https://discord.com/channels/1345804368263385170/1345816347657113702)

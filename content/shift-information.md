# Shift Information

Valley Correctional Facility runs structured roleplay shifts in a private server. Only the **Inmate** team is publicly available; every other position requires whitelisting.

## Schedule & Access

Shifts typically take place on **weekends between 2:00 PM and 5:00 PM EST**. Exact dates and times are announced in **#events**, and the server link is posted in **#shifts** when the session goes live.

:::tip Server Boosters
Server Boosters receive the server link **10–15 minutes early** to guarantee their spot.
:::

## How a Shift Runs

1. **Pre-shift briefing** — Corrections staff arrive 15–20 minutes early for a garage briefing and role assignments.
2. **Inmate processing** — officers move to booking to process inmates into the facility.
3. **Cell-block assignment** — inmates are organised into groups and await the daily schedule.
4. **Daily schedule begins** — leadership announces activities, usually starting with Canteen Time.

## Daily Activities

| Activity | Rules |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Canteen Time** | Orderly meal service; food may not leave the canteen. |
| **Work Time** | Assigned jobs or cell confinement; metal-detector screening is required. |
| **Gym Time** | Gym access or cell time with CO approval; equipment stays in the gym. |
| **Yard Time** | Yard attendance is expected; use equipment appropriately. |
| **Freetime** | Unrestricted movement within non-restricted areas. |

## Teams

### Inmate classifications (no whitelist)
- **Minimum Security** — the default classification.
- **Medium Security** — a disciplinary reclassification.

### Prison staff (whitelist required)
- Corrections Officers
- Medical Team
- CERT (elite tactical unit)
- Riot Control Team

### Law enforcement (whitelist required)
- **Sheriff's Department** — operated by the Moderation Division.
- **Fremont State Police (FSP)** — an independent partner faction.

:::warning Whitelisted teams
Joining any whitelisted team without approval — or exploiting a bug to access one — is a serious offense. See the [community rules](/community-rules/our-rules).
:::

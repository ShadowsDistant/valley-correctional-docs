# Shift Information

Valley Correctional Facility runs structured roleplay shifts in a private server. Only the **Inmate** team is publicly available; all other positions require whitelisting.

## Schedule & Access

Shifts typically occur on **weekends between 2:00 PM and 5:00 PM EST**.

:::tip Server Boosters
Server Boosters receive the server link **10–15 minutes early** to guarantee their spot.
:::

## Shift Process

The facility follows a four-step procedure:

1. **Pre-Shift Briefing** — Corrections team members arrive 15–20 minutes early for a garage briefing and role assignments.
2. **Inmate Processing** — Officers move to booking to process inmates into the facility.
3. **Cell Block Assignment** — Inmates are organised into groups awaiting the daily schedule.
4. **Daily Schedule Begins** — Leadership announces activities, typically starting with Canteen Time.

## Daily Activities

| Activity | Rules |
| --- | --- |
| **Canteen Time** | Orderly meal service; food cannot leave the canteen. |
| **Work Time** | Assigned jobs or cell confinement; metal-detector screening required. |
| **Gym Time** | Gym access or cell time with CO approval; equipment stays in the gym. |
| **Yard Time** | Yard attendance expected; use equipment appropriately. |
| **Freetime** | Unrestricted movement in non-restricted areas. |

## Team Structure

### Inmate Classifications (no whitelist)
- **Minimum Security** — default classification.
- **Medium Security** — disciplinary reclassification.

### Prison Staff (whitelist required)
- Corrections Officers
- Medical Team
- CERT (elite tactical unit)
- Riot Control Team

### Law Enforcement (whitelist required)
- **Sheriff's Department** — operated by the Moderation Division.
- **Fremont State Police (FSP)** — independent partner faction.

## Ground rules (interim)

- All trading is **in-character** and must follow the roleplay rules — no powergaming outcomes, no real-money trading, and no OOC coercion.
- Contraband roleplay is subject to staff direction and may be limited or ended at any time.
- Exploiting bugs to duplicate or obtain items is prohibited and reported to SID.

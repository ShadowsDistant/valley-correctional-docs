# ToS Enforcement Changes

To protect our community, we have deployed **TASE**, a Discord moderation bot that identifies and removes users linked to Roblox "condo" communities. This activity violates Roblox's Terms of Service and frequently involves underage users. TASE is now active and enforces bans according to the criteria below.

***

## What TASE Does

- **Detects accounts linked to condo communities.**
- **Issues bans** when a user meets our enforcement thresholds.
- **Sends an explanatory DM** with appeal instructions when a ban is applied.

***

## Enforcement Criteria

| Criterion | What it means |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Present in more than one condo server** | The user appears in multiple servers known for condo activity |
| **Staff or booster in a condo server** | The user holds a position of influence or support in a condo server |
| **Threat level Critical, High, or Medium** | TASE's risk assessment flags the account at these levels |
| **Score over 200** | The user's score exceeds 200 — 50 points below the permanent, unappealable ban threshold |

***

## What to Expect

- We have already removed **over 120 users** and will continue enforcement.
- **At the end of the month**, the staged criteria will be removed and **anyone involved with condos will be banned**, regardless of the degree of involvement.
- If you are banned from Valley Correctional Facility, you will receive a DM from **TASE** explaining the reason and how to appeal.
- **Appeals must be submitted through TASE.** We do not accept appeals sent directly to staff. Appeal via the TASE instructions or this invite: [discord.gg/VH4e8Wxfmd](https://discord.gg/VH4e8Wxfmd).

***

## Recommendations

- **If you are in any condo server, leave now.** We will not tolerate behaviour that harms children or violates platform rules.
- If you believe a ban was applied in error, follow the appeal instructions sent by TASE.
- For questions about enforcement or community safety, contact server moderators through the usual channels.

Thank you for helping keep our community safe and compliant with Roblox's Terms of Service.

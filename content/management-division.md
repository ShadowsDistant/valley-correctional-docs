# Management Division

The Management Division guides, supports, and directs every operational division so that each functions effectively, ethically, and in step with the community's strategic goals.

## Core Principles

### Leadership & accountability
Managers are held to the highest standard of conduct. Their role is to mentor, support, and guide their teams so that community goals are met consistently — and to be held directly accountable for the conduct of the divisions they lead.

### Strategic oversight
The Management Division sets direction and clears obstacles for staff. Managers are expected to be experts in their division's operations and to continuously develop strategies for improvement.

### Inter-divisional collaboration
No division operates in isolation. Managers work closely with one another to keep communication smooth, resolve friction early, and deliver a seamless experience for the community.

## Areas of Oversight

| Division | Responsibility |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Moderation Division** | Rule enforcement, moderator recruitment, and training |
| **Specialized Investigations Division** | Investigative standards and confidentiality protocols |
| **Prison Administration Division** | Department of Corrections operations and shift management |
| **Development Division** | Development lifecycle and technical project delivery |

## Leadership

- **Community Manager** — holds final authority on community-wide direction.
- **Division Managers** — lead Prison Administration, Internal Operations, and Development respectively.

Major policy changes, handbook amendments, and significant hiring decisions are subject to review by the **Oversight Committee** before taking effect.

:::warning Contacting Management
Please **do not** direct-message managers with support issues. Raise them through the **support ticket system** so they can be tracked and handled by the correct team.
:::
